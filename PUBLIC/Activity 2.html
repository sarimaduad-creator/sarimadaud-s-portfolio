"Churros ssob"!! RECIPE:

Classic Homemade Churros:

  • prep time: 10 mins
  • cook time: 10 mins
  • yields: Approx. 10-12 churros (6-inch)

Ingredients for Dough:

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1-2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla ectract (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon to 2 tablespoon white granulated sugar

Dipping Sauce Ingredients:

  • Chocolate: cooking chocolate or chocolate chips
  • Cream/Milk: heavy cream or milk

Essential Equipment:

  • Piping bag
  • Star tip
  • Small saucepan
  • Deep pot or Dutch oven
  • Thermometer
  • Kitechen scissors
  • Slotted spoon or Spider Strainer
  • Paper towels/wire rack

Procedures:

    1. create a simple dough
    2. piping it into hot oil
    3. frying until golden
    4. and coating it in cinnamon sugar
